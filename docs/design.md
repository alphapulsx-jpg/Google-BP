# Design: GBP Sprint Micro-Offer

## North star
- **Revenue goal:** ~$500 in first 30 days → target **3× $197** upfront.
- **Time budget:** ≤ **[hours/week]**; work batched **after job / weekends**.
- **Interaction model:** **Async only** — no sales calls; **email + forms + payment link**.

## Offer (fixed scope)
- **Name:** $197 Google Business Profile (GBP) Revamp — **7 business days**
- **ICP:** **[Trade]** owners in **[City/region]**, **small to medium businesses under 100 employees**, already serving customers, **weak GBP** (thin profile) OR strong offline signals but **missing/duplicated GBP** (handled cautiously in scoring).
- **Included:** categories/services cleanup, description, attributes where applicable, photo guidance checklist, Q&A prompts, 5 post ideas, competitor positioning notes (light), before/after screenshots, **1 Google Doc deliverable** from template.
- **Excluded:** website rebuild, ads, SEO content machine, “#1 ranking” guarantees, duplicate suppression legal claims, phone support.

## Pricing & policy
- **Price:** $197 **upfront** (Stripe Payment Link).
- **Refund:** Full refund only if **GBP manager access** cannot be granted within **7 days** of payment.

## SLA
- **Start:** Within **48h** of paid intake (weekdays).
- **Delivery:** **7 business days** from start.
- **Your responses:** **[e.g. Tue/Thu 6–7pm + Sun]** email only; no calls.

## Tools (no custom app)
- Carrd or Google Sites (landing)
- Stripe Payment Link
- Tally or Google Forms (intake)
- Google Docs + Drive (deliverable)
- Google Sheet (pipeline)
- Google Places API **or** permitted export + manual review (see `lead-score.md`)

## Metrics
- Landing: visits → payment
- Ops: paid → access granted → delivered on time
- Growth: leads/week, message reply rate, **pay rate**

## Agent / builder rules
- Do **not** expand scope beyond this doc.
- Any new automation must **reduce hours** or **increase payment conversion** — otherwise defer.
