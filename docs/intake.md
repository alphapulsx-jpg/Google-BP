# Intake form (post-payment)

## Tool
- **Tally / Google Forms** — link only **after** Stripe payment (redirect URL).

## Required fields
1. Business legal name + DBA
2. **Google Maps / GBP link** (preferred) **or** business name + city if the link is unknown
3. Service address(es) + **service area** (zip/cities)
4. Top **10 services** (exact phrases customers use)
5. Primary phone + monitoring email
6. Hours + holiday exceptions
7. Brand voice: **Professional / Friendly / Premium / Budget** (pick one)
8. Photo permission: yes/no + link to Drive folder if uploading

## Optional fields
- Competitor names (up to 3)
- **Up to 3 screenshots** of their listing if it is hard to find or verify
- Notes on duplicate / claimed-listing issues (no legal promises)

## Not required
- **No** Google Business Profile manager invite
- **No** shared login — you never access their listing

## Stop / disqualify (auto-reply template)
- Wrong geography → refund.
- Wrong industry (outside ICP) → refund.
- Cannot identify the correct listing after reasonable effort + optional screenshots → refund or manual follow-up per your policy.

## Auto-response email (short)
- Thanks + **what happens next** + **delivery in 48 hours** (after this form) + reminder that they will **apply all changes** using the kit on their schedule + link to upload optional photos/screenshots.
- Remind them the form should take **~5 minutes**; no manager login required.
