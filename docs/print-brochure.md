# Printing the sample leaflet

Open the landing page locally, scroll to **Print this leaflet**, and click **Print sample leaflet** (or use the browser’s Print command). Choose **Save as PDF** or a home/office printer on **US Letter** paper with margins at default (~0.5 in). Enable **Background graphics** so teal, gold, and dashed tear lines print correctly. The PDF is a 3-page leave-behind: cover, sample results + photo strip, and three tear-off cards at the bottom for Maps links, booking, and referrals.
